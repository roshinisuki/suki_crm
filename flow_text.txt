END-TO-END PROCESS FLOW — VARIANT 2 PROFESSIONAL CRMFrom Lead Created to Quotation AcceptedThis flow traces a single industrial deal — a customer inquiry for machined/engineered components — through every module of the Professional CRM, from the moment a Lead is captured to the moment the customer accepts the final Quotation. It shows the realistic manufacturing B2B path: a Lead is qualified, converted into an Account and Opportunity, technically evaluated through Activities, Tasks, Follow Ups, and a Customer Visit, formally priced through an RFQ and costing cycle, and closed through a Quotation. Every step lists the module used, data entered, status changes, records created, automation triggered, notifications sent, and database tables updated.

Each module below is documented across all 15 requirement dimensions: Purpose, Use Case, Database Tables & Fields, Status Workflow, Module Relationships, Automation Rules, Validation Rules, User Roles, Dashboard KPIs, List View Actions, Detail View Actions, Filters, Search Fields, and Bulk Actions.1. DashboardMODULE SUB-VIEWS: My Dashboard   |   Sales Manager Dashboard1. Purpose of ModuleGive every user a single screen that answers &apos;What must I work on today, and how is my territory/business performing?&apos; without navigating into individual modules. My Dashboard is a personal, action-oriented view for Sales Executives; Sales Manager Dashboard is a team-wide, performance-oriented view for supervisors.2. Real Business Use Case (Manufacturing B2B)A Sales Executive selling industrial pumps logs in at 9 AM and sees 3 overdue follow-ups, 2 RFQs awaiting costing input, and 1 quotation expiring today — all before opening any other module. Meanwhile the Regional Sales Manager opens the Sales Manager Dashboard to see that the Chennai territory is at 62% of its monthly target with 6 days left, and that 2 high-value quotations (>₹10L) are stuck in &apos;Sent&apos; status for over 10 days.3. LeadsMODULE SUB-VIEWS: All Leads   |   New Leads   |   Today&apos;s Follow Up   |   SQL   |   Overdue Leads   |   Lost Leads   |   Duplicate Leads1. Purpose of ModuleCapture, qualify, and track every inbound/outbound prospect from first contact until it is either converted into an Account & Opportunity or marked Lost, ensuring no industrial buyer inquiry is missed and that Sales Qualified Leads (SQL) are handed to the right executive quickly.2. Real Business Use Case (Manufacturing B2B)A purchase engineer from an auto-ancillary plant fills a &apos;Request a Quote&apos; form on the website for CNC machine tooling. The lead lands in &apos;New Leads&apos;, gets auto-assigned to the territory-mapped Sales Executive, who calls within 2 hours, qualifies budget/authority/need/timeline, and converts it to SQL — triggering an Account + Contact + Opportunity creation in one click.3.
